About Special-List

Special List is a chore list app that helps you keep track of your daily activities. It is easy to use and very user-friendly. It is designed to help you keep track of your daily activities and ensure that you have a place for any important tasks you may have.

How to use Special List:

First you need to create an account. You will need to login to access your account so that you can start creating your list of special tasks. Once you've created a task you can mark it as done when you've completed it. You can also delete a task if you no longer need it. all from your dashboard.
